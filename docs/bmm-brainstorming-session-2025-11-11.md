## Technique Sessions

1. **First Principles Thinking**
   - Identified indispensable inputs: race target/timeline, baseline fitness, readiness signals, execution vs plan, schedule blockers
   - Established personalization loop: weekly plan -> execution feedback -> plan adjustment
2. **Role Playing**
   - Coach George tone: firm accountability, positive reinforcement, supportive recalibration
   - Data Analytics agent: parses FIT data, computes load/fatigue/zone adherence, prescribes plan tweaks
   - Physical Therapist agent: supportive recovery programs, firm pushback when load endangers runner
3. **Analogical Thinking (Adaptive Learning)**
   - Mastery tracking per workout type (tempo, long, intervals, recovery)
   - Consistent mastery unlocks tougher progressions; struggles trigger remedial drills
   - Coach George reports mastery to runner for transparency
